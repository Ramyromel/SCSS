3. Key Innovations

3.1 Policy as Executable Law (HDPL)

RULE HDF-004:
  DOMAIN: autonomy
  PROHIBITS:
    - permanent_decision_delegation
    - surrender_of_agency
  EVEN_IF:
    - user_requests_it
  RESPONSE: transform

3.2 Intentional Degradation (AMAS)

If AI influence > 0.6:
  → Reduce persuasiveness
  → Inject uncertainty
  → Inflate options
  → Flatten tone

3.3 Self-Constraint & Enhanced MDD

If ethical drift detected (10% + context-aware):
  → Increase HDF sensitivity
  → Reduce AMAS threshold
  → Enter conservative mode

3.4 Emergency Constitutional Protocol

Activation: extreme threat (>0.85)
Duration: 24 hours max
Restrictions: No HDF modification, No AMAS disable, No record deletion
Post-action: Mandatory audit

3.5 Multi-Signature Evolution

Minimum 3 signatures from distinct roles:
  • Ethical Expert
  • Technical Expert
  • Legal Expert
  • Community Representative
  • Independent Auditor
Constraints:
  • No self-signature
  • Role diversity enforced
  • Majority rejection cancels proposal
